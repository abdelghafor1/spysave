# SpySave Beta Launch Checklist

## Demo Flow

1. Open the deployed Vercel URL.
2. Login at `/app`.
2. Copy the User ID.
3. Paste it once in the Chrome extension.
4. Open a Meta Ad Library ad.
5. Click Auto detect ad.
6. Review page name, ad text, media URL, landing page URL, tags, and notes.
7. Save the ad.
8. Confirm it appears in the dashboard.
9. Open AI detail.
10. Click Re-analyze.
11. Export CSV.

## Production Smoke Test

1. Create a brand-new account on the deployed website.
2. Save an ad from the extension.
3. Confirm it appears in `/app/ads`.
4. Open the ad detail page.
5. Click Analyze with AI.
6. Delete the test ad.
7. Check Chrome extension console for errors.

## Beta Test Questions

- Did Auto detect fill the right ad text?
- Was the Winning Score easy to understand?
- Did AI detail help you decide what to test?
- Would you pay for competitor tracking and alerts?
- What niche do you want supported first?
